Few Words About Us

Established in 1997, with headquarters in Al Ain, U.A.E, Super Quality Est. provides superior quality water treatment services serving both the domestic and international sectors. Our goal is to exceed the expectations of every client by offering outstanding customer service, increased flexibility, and greater value, thus offering a wide array of services that suit every sector. Our employees are distinguished by their functional and technical expertise combined with their hands-on experience, thereby ensuring that our clients receive the most effective and professional service.

Super Quality

As experts in water quality treatment, Super Quality Trading Est. is involved in every stage of the water field from reserve osmosis to the selection of water and beverage machinery. Our extensive skills encompass all aspects of implementation and operation, including water treatment and purification, chemicals for water treatment, and pipe fitting. Also, various types of membranes, filters, water pumps, water valves and meters are an integral part of our operations.
